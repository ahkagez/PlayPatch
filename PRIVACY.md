# PlayPatch — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 26 June 2026_

PlayPatch is a browser extension that enhances the YouTube website. This page
explains how it handles your data. In short: it doesn't collect any.

## Data collection

PlayPatch does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are
no servers, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

## Settings storage

Your preferences — which features are enabled, the accent color, the interface
language, and the popup theme — are saved **locally** in your browser using the
extension storage API. This data never leaves your device and is used only to
remember your settings between sessions. You can export, import, or reset it at
any time from the extension's settings.

## Permissions

- **storage** — to save your settings locally on your device.
- **Access to `*.youtube.com`** — the extension only runs on YouTube, where it
  modifies the page to apply the features you enable. It does not run on, or
  read, any other website.

## Third parties

PlayPatch does not share data with anyone and does not send data anywhere.
